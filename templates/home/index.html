Do you know?

What actually do you drink and what is the diffrence between juice, nectar and drink?

Juice

Juice 100% FRUIT INTAKE juice squeezed directly from the fruit or obtained from concentrated fruit juice by recreating the proportion of water and aroma removed during the thickening process the juice does not contain any artificial colors or flavors; cannot be chemically preserved.

Nectar

Nectar FROM 25 TO 99% OF THE FRUIT INPUT diluted juice with water with added sugar, possibly citric acid The nectar does not contain artificial colors or flavors; cannot be chemically preserved.

Drink

Drink FROM 20 TO 99% OF THE FRUIT INPUT contains a certain amount of juice, water, sugar, acid lemons and other permitted food additives The drink may contain dyes and aromas and others additives.
